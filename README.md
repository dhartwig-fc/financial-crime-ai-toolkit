# financial-crime-ai-toolkit
A portfolio of AI-enabled Financial Crime prototypes demonstrating alert triage, investigator copilots, network analytics and workflow optimisation across AML, TBML, correspondent banking and capital markets compliance.

The Financial Crime AI Transformation Toolkit is a collection of practical prototypes demonstrating how artificial intelligence, analytics and workflow automation can enhance Financial Crime Operations.
The toolkit explores key transformation themes including:
	●	AML Alert Triage & Risk Scoring
	●	AI Investigator Copilots
	●	Network Analytics & Entity Resolution
	●	Beneficial Ownership Analysis
	●	Correspondent Banking Risk Assessment
	●	Trade-Based Money Laundering (TBML) Analytics
	●	Capital Markets Surveillance
	●	Adverse Media Intelligence
	●	SAR Narrative Drafting
	●	Case Management Optimisation
The objective is not to replicate enterprise platforms, but to demonstrate how modern AI and analytics techniques can augment investigator workflows, improve risk prioritisation and support more effective financial crime controls.
